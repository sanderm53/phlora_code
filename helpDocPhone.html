Phlora help

Navigation (DEPRECATED!):
  • Pinch or swipe tree to zoom or pan
  • Tap any gray dot, if present, to open image (tap again to close)
  • Swipe across gray dots to briefly open images
  • Pinch or swipe image, once open, to zoom or pan
  • Double tap image, once open, to maximize or minimize
  • Triple tap image, once open, to freeze it in place or unfreeze (by default, image pans with tree)
