Phlora help

Navigation:
  • Pinch or swipe tree to zoom or pan
  • Tap any gray dot, if present, to open image (tap again to close)
  • Pinch or swipe image, once open, to zoom or pan
  • Double tap image, once open, to increase in size
Toolbar buttons:
  • Yellow-Green button (if present): toggle classification
  • White button (if present): toggle image display after images are opened
